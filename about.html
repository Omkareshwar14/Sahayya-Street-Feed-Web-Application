Description

Welcome to Sahayya Street Feed, the web application that connects you with the best local street vendors in your area. Our mission is to promote small businesses and foster a sense of community by providing a platform for users to discover and connect with local vendors.

With Sahayya Street Feed, users can easily find and browse local vendors, view their menus or products, and connect with them directly to place orders or make purchases.
